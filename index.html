About Us

Discover the story behind CozyCupCafé.

LAVA JAVA Café is known as JAVA café. We started building on June 11, 2018. We began as a small business, creating simple coffees like mocha and chocolate. In 2019, milk became popular because we added a twist (ice coffee flavor: milk with strawberry toppings) during the summer season. In the year 2020, during the pandemic, we created a social media platform for delivery and online orders. We started adding new styles, flavors, and upgrades for our coffees.
